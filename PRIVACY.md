# Vaporcast Privacy Policy

  _Last updated: April 18, 2026_

  Vaporcast is designed to work without collecting your personal data.

  ## What Vaporcast does NOT do

  - No accounts or sign-ins
  - No third-party SDKs of any kind — no analytics, no telemetry, no
    crash reporting, no advertising, no attribution, no A/B testing frameworks
  - No data is sent to any server operated by the developer
  - No data is sold, shared, or licensed to third parties

  ## What Vaporcast uses (all first-party Apple services)

  **Apple WeatherKit** — Vaporcast sends your Apple TV's approximate
  location to Apple's WeatherKit service to fetch the local forecast.
  This is handled by Apple under their privacy policy:
  https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

  **Apple Music (optional)** — If you choose to play background music,
  Vaporcast uses MusicKit and plays audio from your Apple Music
  subscription. Apple handles this data under their own policy. If you
  do not have Apple Music or decline the permission, Vaporcast works
  silently and does not contact Apple Music.

  **Location Services** — Vaporcast requests "when in use" location
  access to determine your local weather. On Apple TV this is an
  IP-based approximation. The location is only used locally and is not
  transmitted anywhere except to Apple's WeatherKit service above.

  ## Children's privacy

  Vaporcast is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly
  collect any personal data from anyone.

  ## Changes

  If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will
  replace this file.
