# Vaporcast Support

  Vaporcast is a retro weather dashboard for Apple TV — a love letter
  to the 1990s Weather Channel broadcast era, fused with vaporwave
  aesthetics. It runs in your living room and shows current weather,
  forecasts, almanac, moon, barometer, and more on rotating panels.

  ## Report a bug or request a feature

  Please open an issue here:
  👉 https://github.com/klobucar/vaporcast-support/issues

  When reporting a bug, it helps to include:
  - Apple TV model (HD, 4K 1st gen, 4K 2nd/3rd gen)
  - tvOS version (Settings → General → About)
  - What you were doing when the bug happened

  ## FAQ

  **Do I need Apple Music to use Vaporcast?**
  No. Music is optional. Without it, Vaporcast shows weather in silence
  — or lets your other audio apps (Spotify, AirPlay, etc.) keep playing
  in the background.

  **What data does Vaporcast collect?**
  None. Vaporcast stores nothing and sends no analytics. Weather comes
  from Apple WeatherKit based on your Apple TV's location.

  **Why is the weather slow to update?**
  Vaporcast refreshes every 15 minutes automatically, plus once
  whenever the app returns to the foreground. If WeatherKit is having
  a rough day, it retries with backoff.
